Chef's salat

A very flexible recipe.
Prepare the ingredients as you like, mix with sauce.
Enjoy a quick and healthy starter.

Way of cooking

It's a very simple recipe and you can freestyle your ingredients list according to your taste. Get ingredients ready, and mix them. That's all you need to do.

Ingredients

A package of rocket lettuce.
A few cherry tomatoes, quartered.
2 eggs, hard boiled, peeled and quartered.
A few ham accourding to your favor.
Cheddar cheese.
Cider vinegar, honey, olive oil, salt and pepper.
